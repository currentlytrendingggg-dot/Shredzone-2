Unlock the Power of BMR

Your basal metabolic rate represents the energy required to keep essential body functions running at rest. Understanding your BMR helps calibrate calories for precise body composition change.

  • Align caloric intake to your recovery demands
  • Ensure deficits never exceed 20% of TDEE
  • Pair with lab work for thyroid and hormonal markers
