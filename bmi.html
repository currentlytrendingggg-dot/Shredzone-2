When BMI Works Best

BMI is a quick screening tool for most adults. It correlates strongly with metabolic risk when combined with waist circumference, activity level, and blood panels.

  • General wellness and medical screenings
  • Corporate wellness and health risk assessments
  • Population-level research and case management

When BMI Falls Short

BMI does not directly measure body composition. Athletes, pregnant clients, and those with high muscle mass need a more comprehensive evaluation.

  • DEXA or InBody on-site at our lab partners
  • Caliper testing or circumference protocol
  • Blood biomarkers & metabolic testing
